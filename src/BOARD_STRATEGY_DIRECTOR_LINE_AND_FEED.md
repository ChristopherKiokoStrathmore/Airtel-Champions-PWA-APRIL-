**GDPR/Privacy Compliance:**

2. ✅ **Auto-Blur Faces**: AI blurs faces unless explicitly allowed
3. ✅ **No Phone Numbers**: Auto-redact customer phone numbers
4. ✅ **Location Privacy**: Only show zone, not exact GPS
5. ✅ **Delete Option**: SEs can delete their posts anytime
6. ✅ **Report Button**: Flag inappropriate content

**Content Moderation:**
- 🤖 **AI Moderation**: Flag profanity, competitor names, sensitive data
- 👮 **Human Review**: Christopher reviews flagged posts
- ⚠️ **Warning System**: 1st offense = warning, 3rd = suspension
- 🚫 **Banned Content**: Violence, discrimination, harassment

**Brand Protection:**
- ✅ All photos watermarked "TAI by Airtel"
- ✅ Posts cannot be exported/downloaded
- ✅ External sharing disabled (only within TAI)
- ✅ Screenshot detection (notify admins)
