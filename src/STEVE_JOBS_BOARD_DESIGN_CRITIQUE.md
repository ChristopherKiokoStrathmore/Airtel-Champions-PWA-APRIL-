>

**Jony:**
> "And these colors. Airtel Red #E20613. Beautiful. Passionate. But you're using it... everywhere? No.
>
> **Hierarchy of Color:**
>
> **1. Airtel Red (#E20613)** - Hearts, alerts, urgent actions  
> **2. Airtel Green (#00A859)** - Success, points, achievements

## 🧠 **DON NORMAN - USER PSYCHOLOGY**

**Don** *(opens laptop, pulls up research)*

> "Let's talk about what we KNOW from cognitive science:

**1. SWIPE LEFT TO REACH DIRECTOR**

*Shows eye-tracking data*

**Don:**
> "Brilliant affordance. Users already know swipe gestures. BUT - you need a visual cue. Not text that says 'swipe left.' That's lazy.
>
> **Better:** 
> - Subtle animation: screen slightly slides right on its own, then back
> - Shows a peek of orange/red edge on the left
> - Creates curiosity: 'What's over there?'
> - No words needed. The interface teaches itself."

**2. THE RED HEART**

**Don:**
> "Excellent instinct. Red hearts trigger dopamine. Studies show:
> - Red hearts: 34% more engagement than other colors
> - Heart shape: Universal symbol, no translation needed
> - Animation matters: Heart should 'pop' when tapped (scale 0.8x → 1.2x → 1.0x in 300ms)
>
> But here's the key: Don't just say '47 likes.' Say '47 people loved this.' Or better: Show FACES of who liked it."

```
❤️ Ashish, Mary, and 45 others
```

**Don:**
> "Now it's SOCIAL. Now it's HUMAN. Now it matters."

**3. DIRECTOR LINE CATEGORIES**

**Don:**
> "Six choices = cognitive overload. Miller's Law: 7±2 items. You're at the limit.
>
> **Better approach:**
> 
> **Phase 1: What's on your mind?** (One input)  
> **Phase 2: AI suggests category** ('This sounds urgent. Send as priority?')  
> **Phase 3: User confirms or changes**
>
> Three steps. One decision at a time. No paralysis."

**4. FEED SCROLLING**

**Don:**
> "Infinite scroll is addictive. That's not always good. For Instagram, yes. For TAI, you want:
>
> **PURPOSEFUL ENGAGEMENT**
>
> After 10 posts, show: 'You've caught up! 🎉 Time to go sell?'
>
> Why? Because TAI isn't about wasting time. It's about LEARNING, then APPLYING. Read tips. Close app. Sell more. Come back. Share your win. Repeat.
>
> **INTENTIONAL DESIGN**."

## ✨ **MIKE MATAS - INTERACTION & ANIMATION**

**Mike** *(stands up, starts gesturing)*

> "Okay okay okay. Great feedback from everyone. But here's what makes it FEEL magical: ANIMATION.

**SWIPE LEFT TO DIRECTOR LINE:**

*Pulls out phone, demonstrates*

**Mike:**
> "Watch. You swipe left. The screen doesn't just slide. It PEELS. Like turning a page. The edge lifts slightly. There's a shadow. The Director Line screen has DEPTH. It exists BEHIND the main screen. You're not switching screens. You're REVEALING what was always there."

*Shows prototype*

**Mike's Animation Specs:**

```
SWIPE LEFT TRANSITION:
- Duration: 400ms
- Easing: Cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1) [Material Design]  
- Effect: 3D perspective transform (rotateY)
- Shadow: Dynamic shadow follows the peel
- Haptic: Light feedback when swipe completes (iOS) / Vibrate 50ms (Android)
```

**LIKE BUTTON ANIMATION:**

**Mike:**
> "When someone taps the heart, don't just turn it red. Make it BURST with joy:"

```
HEART ANIMATION:
Frame 1 (0ms):    Scale 1.0, Gray
Frame 2 (50ms):   Scale 0.8, Gray (anticipation)
Frame 3 (100ms):  Scale 1.3, Red (explosion!)
Frame 4 (200ms):  Scale 1.0, Red (settle)

+ Particle effect: 6 tiny hearts explode outward (120ms)
+ Haptic: Medium feedback
```

**POST APPEARANCE:**

**Mike:**
> "New posts don't just appear. They ARRIVE:"

```
NEW POST ANIMATION:
- Slides in from top
- Slight bounce at the end (spring physics)
- Glow effect for first 2 seconds
- Then settles into the feed

Duration: 600ms
Spring: Tension 300, Friction 20
```

**DIRECTOR COMMENT NOTIFICATION:**

**Mike:**
> "When ASHISH comments on YOUR post? That's special. That deserves special treatment:"

```
DIRECTOR COMMENT:
- Phone vibrates (pattern: short, short, LONG)
- Notification has GOLD shimmer animation
- Crown icon pulses 3 times
- Sound: Premium notification chime (not default)
```

**Mike:**
> "Every interaction should feel ALIVE. Not software. ALIVE."
