## User Journey

### Sales Executive Journey
```
1. Open App
   ↓
2. Tap "Programs" tab
   ↓
3. See folders with programs
   ↓
4. Tap "💰 Sales Programs" to expand
   ↓
5. See 3 sales programs
   ↓
6. Tap "Shop Visit"
   ↓
7. Fill submission form
   ↓
8. Submit & earn 50 points!
   ↓
9. Success modal shows
   ↓
10. Back to programs list
```

### HQ Director Journey
```
1. Open App
   ↓
2. Tap "Programs" tab
   ↓
3. Toggle to "Analytics" mode
   ↓
4. See [ANALYTICS] badges on programs
   ↓
5. Tap "Shop Visit"
   ↓
6. See analytics dashboard
   ↓
7. Check Overview: 156 subs, 78 users
   ↓
8. Switch to "Trends" tab
   ↓
9. See daily submission history
   ↓
10. Switch to "Top Performers"
   ↓
11. See 🥇 Jane Smith (25 submissions)
   ↓
12. Identify top performers for rewards
   ↓
13. Close and review next program
```
