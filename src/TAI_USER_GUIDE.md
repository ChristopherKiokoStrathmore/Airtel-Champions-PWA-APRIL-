## 👥 USER ROLES & HIERARCHY

TAI has 5 user roles, each with increasing levels of access:

### **🔴 Level 1: Field Agent (SE)**
**What you can do:**
- ✅ Capture intelligence photos
- ✅ View daily missions
- ✅ Track your streak
- ✅ See leaderboard (all 662 SEs)
- ✅ View your submissions
- ✅ Check your rank

**What you see:**
- Red header bar
- Personal stats (rank, streak)
- Top 3 performers
- Programs to capture
- Announcements

---

### **🔵 Level 2: Zone Commander (ZSM)**
**What you can do (EVERYTHING SE can do, PLUS):**
- ✅ Review submissions from your zone
- ✅ Approve or reject intel
- ✅ See zone statistics (Total SEs, Active Today)
- ✅ View pending reviews queue
- ✅ Monitor zone performance

**What you see:**
- Blue header bar
- Zone dashboard
- Approval/rejection buttons
- Zone leaderboard

---

### **🟢 Level 3: Zone Business Lead (ZBM)**
**What you can do (EVERYTHING ZSM can do, PLUS):**
- ✅ View business metrics (conversion rates, revenue impact)
- ✅ Track ROI from intel submissions
- ✅ Monitor AMB performance
- ✅ Compare zones

**What you see:**
- Green header bar
- Business metrics dashboard
- Revenue analytics

---

### **🟣 Level 4: HQ Command Center**
**What you can do (EVERYTHING ZBM can do, PLUS):**
- ✅ National overview (all 662 SEs)
- ✅ Create/edit programs
- ✅ Send national announcements
- ✅ Manage user assignments
- ✅ Export reports

**What you see:**
- Purple header bar
- National dashboard
- Program management tools
- User management interface

### **🟡 Level 5: Director (S&D Director)**
**What you can do (EVERYTHING + GOD MODE):**
- ✅ Adjust point weights for programs

## 🔄 HOW TO SWITCH ROLES

1. **Click your profile icon** (top right, red circle with your initial)
2. **See the dropdown menu:**
   - Your name & employee ID
   - Zone, ZSM, ZBM info
   - **"Switch View" section** with 5 roles
3. **Click any role** to switch instantly:
   - ○ Field Agent (SE) - Red dot
   - ○ Zone Commander (ZSM) - Blue dot
   - ○ Zone Business Lead (ZBM) - Green dot
   - ○ HQ Command Center - Purple dot
   - ○ Director - Yellow dot
4. **Current role** shows a checkmark ✓

### **Visual Indicators:**
- Header color changes based on role
- Role badge shows in header
- You'll never lose track of which view you're in!

## 📊 KEY FEATURES

### **🔥 Daily Missions**
- See purple card at top of home screen
- Example: "Capture 3 Network Experience photos"
- Progress bar shows completion (e.g., 1/3)
- Points reward displayed (e.g., +50 pts)

### **🔥 Streak Counter**
- Fire emoji + number in header
- Shows consecutive days of activity
- Example: "🔥 7 day streak"
- Don't break your streak!

### **📡 Offline Queue**
- Yellow banner appears when submissions are offline
- Shows number waiting to sync
- "Sync Now" button to upload when online
- Disappears when queue is empty

### **🏆 Leaderboard**
- Shows all 662 SEs ranked
- Top 3 highlighted (Gold, Silver, Bronze)
- Your rank always visible in header
- Click "View All" to see full ranking

### **📢 Announcements**
- Blue megaphone icon (top right)
- Red badge shows unread count
- Click to see all announcements
- From S&D Director and leadership

### **📊 Programs (4 Types)**
1. **📶 Network Experience** (Blue card)
2. **🎯 Competition Conversion** (Green card)
3. **🚀 New Site Launch** (Purple card)
4. **🏢 AMB Visitation** (Orange card)
